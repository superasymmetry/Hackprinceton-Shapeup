The Problem

Haircut regret is a billion-dollar
industry.

I · Uncertainty

You can't picture it on your own head.

Reference photos lie. Your hairline, your face shape, your head size — they rewrite every cut.

II · Translation

Barbers are guessing what you want.

"Kinda like this but shorter" sends thousands of cuts sideways every weekend.

III · Iteration

There is no undo button for hair.

Six weeks of regrowth is a terrible feedback loop for a fifty-dollar decision.

05
Deep Tech

Three things we're quietly proud of.

i.

PCA-guided style matching.

We decomposed 343 studio-scanned hairstyles into three interpretable axes — width, length, depth. Your head is a point in the same space. The closest point is your next haircut.

PCA · nearest neighbor
ii.

A hair model we actually trained.

Our in-house generator mixes your face with the candidate hair. When a prompt lives in the long tail, we fall through to Gemini — so the demo never breaks.

In-house · Gemini fallback
iii.

Volumetric 3D in the browser.

No pre-baked turntable. Gaussian splats stream live into React Three Fiber, so you can drag your head around like a Figma component.

WebGL · live splat
